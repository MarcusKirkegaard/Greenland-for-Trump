About us

As soon as we heard that the President of the United States was thinking about buying Greenland, we considered the possibilities it would bring to our country – and we didn’t think twice. The following morning, we started the Trump for Greenland foundation, a non-profit organization with one goal: Seal the deal and let Donald Trump buy our country with all that it entails.

Since then our organization has been ever growing, with demonstrations, rallies, speakers and over 60.000 people have now signed our most recent petition.

We are not going to stop until we reach our goals and make Greenland great again.

- Marcus Kirkegaard, founder of the Greenland for Trump foundation
